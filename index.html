Our calling

We go so Thailand may know

Our mission in Thailand is to share the Gospel, disciple believers, and partner with local churches—training leaders and meeting practical needs in Thai communities.

  • ✝

    Gospel-centered

    Every effort is rooted in the good news of Jesus Christ and the authority of Scripture.

  • 🇹🇭

    In Thailand

    We live and serve among the Thai people, learning language and culture to share Christ in meaningful ways.

  • 🤝

    Partnership

    We work alongside Thai churches and believers, not in place of them—building up the body of Christ in Thailand.
