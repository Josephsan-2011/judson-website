Faith & practice

Our Beliefs

We hold to the historic Christian faith as revealed in Scripture.

We believe in one God eternally existing in three persons (Father, Son, and Holy Spirit); in the Bible as the authoritative Word of God; and in salvation by grace alone through faith alone in Jesus Christ alone. We serve Christ and His church in light of the Great Commission. Our Beliefs

Good news

The Gospel (Romans Road)

The path from sin to eternal life through Jesus Christ, as shown in the book of Romans.

  1. Romans 3:23 — All have sinned and fall short of God’s glory.
  2. Romans 6:23 — The wages of sin is death; God’s gift is eternal life in Christ.
  3. Romans 5:8 — God demonstrates His love: Christ died for us while we were still sinners.
  4. Romans 10:9–10 — Confess Jesus as Lord, believe God raised Him from the dead, and you will be saved.
  5. Romans 10:13 — Everyone who calls on the name of the Lord will be saved.

What we do

Ministries

Our mission in Myanmar and Thailand is to share the Gospel, disciple believers, and partner with local churches—training leaders and meeting practical needs in the communities we serve.

  • ✝

    Gospel-centered

    Every effort is rooted in the good news of Jesus Christ and the authority of Scripture.

  • 🌏

    On the field

    We live and serve in Myanmar and Thailand, learning language and culture to share Christ in meaningful ways among the Burmese people and those around us.

  • 🤝

    Partnership

    We work alongside local churches and believers, not in place of them—building up the body of Christ where we serve.
